Share a photo of your favourite Mzansi meal to win!

What better way to launch Heritage Month than to celebrate proudly South African Cuisines which reflect our diversity and uniqueness as a nation. Together with Chef Nthabi, Budget brings you Taste of Mzansi- a proudly South African competition which is sure to get your taste buds titillated.

To stand a chance of being 1 of the 4 lucky winners, all you have to do is share a picture of your favourite Mzansi meal and Chef Nthabi could be driving to your home to prepare this meal exclusively for you.

Mzansi food lovers, this one is for you, so start sharing those pictures!
